Gender-Age Detection

Predict demographic properties from facial features in real-time.

About This Project

This tool utilizes Deep Neural Networks (DNN) trained on computer vision models to estimate a person's age and gender. It processes the image to locate a face using a Single Shot Detector (SSD), and subsequently runs classification networks on the isolated region.

Instructions

  • Upload: Drag and drop a clear, well-lit portrait photo. Avoid group photos for best accuracy.
  • Live Camera: Ensure your face is centered and clearly visible before capturing the photo.
Important Notice Model accuracy heavily depends on image quality. Poor lighting, extreme facial angles, occlusions (like glasses or masks), or multiple faces may result in incorrect predictions or errors. Results should be treated as estimates.
